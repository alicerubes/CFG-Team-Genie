ABOUT ALICE

Hey! I'm Alice. I became interested in coding after watching Mr. Robot and decided to take on the challenge myself. I've always been a jack of all trades, with a mixture of both academic and creative qualities, so I've found it difficult to nail down what career I want or would be good at.

I began a course with Code First Girls and I loved it so much that I am now also doing a few courses with CodeCademy. I hope one day I can switch from my marketing job and move into web development.
